PBXMON / Asterisk Monitor

*monitor is a powerful Real time Asterisk Web Monitor that allows you to monitor your Asterisk system in real time from your desktop or mobile device. *monitor uses Meteor's powerful real time web capabilities, along with Asterisk's AMI interface, to bring you instant page updates as your customers move around your Asterisk system. Watch as new callers dial in, as users join and leave conference bridges, and much more.
